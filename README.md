### What is Tombala?

Tombala is a game where players attempt to match drawn numbers. Players examine numbered balls drawn and try to find matching numbers within a certain pattern.

### Who is Tombalacı Mehmet?

Tombalaci Mehmet is a character from the Turkish TV series "Kurtlar Vadisi". He is a street vendor who runs a Tombala game and is known for his rough exterior, quick wit, and soft heart. Despite his tough persona, he is a beloved character and remembered by fans for his charming personality nature.
